• Fetch The Slider With Multiple Data Sources

    Slidershock is very flexible, that means that you can show YouTube videos, Flickr Images, Instagram images, images taken from a RSS feed. You can also fetch the slider with the content of a Twitter account.

  • What’s Cool

    You can change image dimensions and text ones as well, so that you can have the text in the right and the images in the left or vice versa, on the other hand, you can also have the text floating over the image, in the top, bottom, right, or left.

  • Lots Of Effects To Choose From

    One of the coolest things you can find in SliderShock, is the amount built in effects it has, from simple to complex. Loop through them and find the one you like the most.
